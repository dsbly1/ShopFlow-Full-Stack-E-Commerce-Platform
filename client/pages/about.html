Our Story

Born from a simple idea

ShopFlow was founded in 2026 with one goal in mind: make buying and selling online feel as natural as shopping at your favorite local store — without the friction, the hidden fees, or the clunky interfaces that plague so many marketplaces.

We set out to build a full-stack e-commerce platform from scratch — a real product with real users, real transactions, and real trust at its core. Every feature, from secure authentication to seller onboarding to order tracking, was designed with people first.

Today, ShopFlow is a growing marketplace where anyone can list products, reach customers, and get paid — and where shoppers can discover unique items with confidence.

Milestones

How we got here

Early 2026

Idea & Architecture

ShopFlow began as a blueprint — a full-stack e-commerce platform designed from the ground up with Node.js, PostgreSQL, and a clean vanilla frontend.

Mid 2026

Core Platform Launch

Authentication, product listings, cart, and checkout went live. The seller registration flow and dashboard were built and tested with real data.

Late 2026

Seller Ecosystem & Reviews

Seller onboarding, banking details, order management, and the review system were completed, turning ShopFlow into a true two-sided marketplace.

2027 & Beyond

Growing the Community

ShopFlow continues to grow — improving the buyer experience, expanding seller tools, and welcoming new users to the platform every day.
