U.S. Federal Tax Obligations for Online Sellers

  • IRS Form 1099-K: ShopFlow will issue a 1099-K if you process over $600 in sales in a calendar year (effective 2024). You must report this income on your federal tax return.
  • Self-Employment Tax: Sole proprietors owe 15.3% SE tax on net earnings above $400/year.
  • Sales Tax Nexus: Since the South Dakota v. Wayfair (2018) Supreme Court ruling, states can require out-of-state sellers to collect sales tax once they exceed economic nexus thresholds (typically $100,000 in sales or 200 transactions per state).
  • Resale Certificates: If purchasing wholesale goods for resale, obtain a state resale certificate to avoid paying sales tax on inventory.
  • Home Occupation Permit: Many municipalities require a business license or home occupation permit even for online-only sellers.

Consumer Protection Laws

  • FTC Act Section 5: Prohibits unfair or deceptive acts. Listings must be accurate — no fake reviews, misleading descriptions, or bait-and-switch.
  • Consumer Product Safety Act (CPSA): You may not sell recalled, unsafe, or non-compliant products. Check CPSC.gov before listing any children's products.
  • CAN-SPAM Act: Marketing emails to buyers must include an unsubscribe option and your physical address.
  • GDPR / CCPA: If selling to EU or California residents, you must comply with applicable data privacy laws.

Intellectual Property

  • You may not list counterfeit, replica, or unauthorized reproductions of branded goods.
  • Product photos you upload must be owned by you or properly licensed.
  • Brand names, logos, and trademarks of third parties may not appear in your store name.

ShopFlow Seller Agreement — Summary of Key Terms

This is a plain-language summary. The full legal agreement governs.

  • Commission: ShopFlow charges a 10% referral fee on each completed sale (excluding shipping). This fee is automatically deducted before payout.
  • Payouts: Seller payouts are processed every 14 days to your registered bank account, after any pending disputes are resolved.
  • Listing Standards: Listings must include at least one photo, an accurate description, price, condition, and shipping details. ShopFlow reserves the right to remove non-compliant listings.
  • Condition Definitions: New (sealed/unused), Like New (opened but unused), Excellent (minimal signs of use), Good (normal wear), Fair (functional with visible wear), For Parts (not fully functional).
  • Dispute Resolution: In the event of a buyer dispute, ShopFlow's Trust & Safety team will mediate. Sellers must respond within 48 hours. Unresolved disputes may result in automatic refund to the buyer.
  • Account Suspension: Accounts with an order defect rate above 3%, late shipment rate above 5%, or valid intellectual property complaints will be suspended pending review.
  • Termination: Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Outstanding payouts will be processed at the end of the standard payout cycle.
  • Governing Law: This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, USA.
